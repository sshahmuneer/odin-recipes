Brownies

Description

This simple brownie recipe takes about 30 minutes to make from start to finish! Bake these brownies whenever you need to quickly satisfy your sweet tooth. Made with kitchen staples, you'll come back to this recipe again and again.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 4 eggs
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup walnut halves
